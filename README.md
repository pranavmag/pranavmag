I am deeply passionate about computer architecture, low-level systems programming, and high-performance computing. My journey is defined by a burning passion for bare-metal engineering over high-level abstractions, preferring to build fundamental systems.

I actively share my research, projects, and deep dives into binary logic on my YouTube channel, **Noot Nav**. Join me as we break down the complexities of systems architecture!
